# Introduction

Button is a special widget to execute some action, for example - submit form, stop VPS or go to next  wizard step. Different button types have their own visual styles.

There are three groups of buttons visual look: 
1. Plain button: A typically rectangular material button. Choose it for buttons inside unclickable containers or outside of any container.
2. Regular button: A button made of ink and does not lift. Choose it inside clickable containers, such as [Home Tile](/ui-kit/views/tile/home) or [On-Screen Notification](/app-kit/common/notifications/visualization#on-screen-notifications)
3. Text buttons: TODO

The every button also can be aligned on left, center, or right. 

Also button content can include: Text, Icon, Action menu icon.
