# Design Checklist

## Designing Customer CP Screens for Odin Automation Services
When you design any screen or separate components (tiles, tables, etc.), use this checklist to make sure that you didn't forget something important.

Please note that not all of these points apply to everything: sometimes the Platform does most of the work for you. But generally these points are the ones that worth thinking of.

Ideally, after your design is ready, all checkboxes should be checked.
