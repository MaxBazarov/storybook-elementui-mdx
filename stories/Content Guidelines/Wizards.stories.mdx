# Wizards

For steps titles, use action names (active voice, imperative mood) and capitalize them in book title style. 

For wizard title, use active voice and imperative mood.

For wizard screen description, follow [Introductory or Instructional Text guidelines](../introductory-or-instructional-text).

**Wizard Navigation Buttons:**
* Fist step should include “Cancel” and “Next” button. 
* The middle step should include “Cancel”, “Back” and “Next” button.
* At the last step include “Cancel”, “Back” and final button. Usually, final button is called by "Finish", but in case of purchasing a new service it can be also called “Subscribe”, “Get Trial” or "Buy".
